FAQ:

Where is my data being stored?

Nivix Studio Apps save their data in your system’s home folder:
• Windows: C:\Users\(your_username)\nvxstdo\
• Linux: /home/(your_username)/nvxstdo/


Can I downgrade Studio Apps?

Yes, you can downgrade. All versions of all Nivix Studio apps remain available and usable. Older versions may be missing newer security fixes or features, so use them with caution.
